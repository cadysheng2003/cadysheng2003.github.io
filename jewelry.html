When the stones are taken away from the mountains and rivers of their homeland, can we still feel their souls? My work explores a sense of sacred balance through color, texture, form, and rhythm.

Turquoise, jade, Southern red agate, Alxa sugar-heart agate, moss agate, silver, raw stones, carving, and weaving become the language of this exploration.

Some of my pieces are inspired by Buddhist elements.
